Cybersecurity, Privacy, and UNKnown risks in AI.

CyberPUNK Lab is led by Dr Han Wu at the University of Southampton. We study AI security, privacy, and unknown risks in modern AI systems, connecting foundational research with the practical challenges of building dependable technology.
